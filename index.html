Hello there! My name is Calvin Mitchell, and I'm a fourth-year computer information systems major from Homestead, FL. I've had my hands on the internet and technology for as long as I can remember. My first interaction with any kind of big tech, other than my mom's Samsung Galaxy S III, was a bulky, white Dell(?) computer that my babysitter owned. She couldn't use it much because it used a lot of electricity in their home, so the one video I remember vividly is LMFAO's - I'm Sexy and I Know It on YouTube during its heyday. She showed me more videos and Flash games that could never run on my mom's old laptop. After that, I fell in love with the computer, and it introduced me to a world that I never thought existed in my young mind, and here we are.
